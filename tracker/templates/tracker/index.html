Fixing Windows Location Error

This error often occurs on Windows because the system's location features are disabled.

  • Go to Settings > Privacy & security > Location.
  • Ensure Location services is On.
  • Ensure Let apps access your location is On.
  • Ensure Let desktop apps access your location is On (specifically for your browser).
